Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At MedEcho AI, we prioritize the protection of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our educational simulation platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you register, we collect your name, email address, professional title, and training level to customize your dashboard and simulation difficulty.
Simulation Data: We store the chat transcripts, audio recordings, and feedback scores generated during your practice sessions to allow you to review your history.
Payment Information: All payments are processed securely by our third-party provider (Stripe). We do not store or process your credit card information on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve the MedEcho AI service, personalize the educational feedback you receive, process transactions, and send you important technical notices regarding your account.

3. Third-Party AI Services

MedEcho AI relies on external APIs to generate content. When you engage in a consultation, your text inputs and audio recordings are transmitted to OpenAI (for natural language processing and grading) and ElevenLabs (for voice synthesis). These providers process the data solely to generate the response and do not use MedEcho API data to train their public models.

4. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your account. Your practice history is retained so you can track your progress over time. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data rights, please contact us at support@medecho.ai.
