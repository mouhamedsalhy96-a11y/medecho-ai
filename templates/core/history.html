Origin Story

Built by a doctor,
for the next generation.

The story of MedEcho AI begins with a common, frustrating bottleneck shared by thousands of international medical graduates aiming to practice in the UK.

The PLAB Challenge

After graduating from medical school in 2023, Dr. Mohamed Salhi relocated to the United Kingdom, settling in Cumbria. While actively working as a Healthcare Assistant to support himself and familiarize himself with the NHS system, he began preparing for the PLAB examinations—a notoriously rigorous assessment of clinical and communication skills.

He quickly realized that while medical knowledge can be learned from textbooks, consultation skills require active muscle memory. Passing OSCE-style exams like PLAB 2 demands empathy, active listening, and the ability to think on your feet. The only way to improve is through repeated practice with a partner.

But coordinating schedules with other busy doctors across different time zones was nearly impossible. Roleplaying with non-medical friends lacked clinical accuracy. He found himself wishing for a 24/7 practice partner—a patient who was always available, clinically accurate, and capable of giving instant, objective feedback.

Medicine Meets Code

Most doctors would have simply pushed through the frustration. But alongside his medical degree, Dr. Salhi had spent years mastering full-stack software development. Fluent in modern web frameworks, AI integrations, and backend architecture, he realized he possessed the exact skill set required to build the tool he so desperately needed.

He opened his code editor and went to work. What began as a personal utility—a simple prompt engine to simulate patient responses—rapidly evolved. He integrated state-of-the-art Large Language Models to handle complex medical histories. He added live voice processing so he could put his keyboard away and practice speaking out loud. He even built a dynamic scoring system to critique his management plans against strict clinical guidelines.

The Birth of MedEcho AI

When the system was complete, the results were transformative. The ability to simulate high-pressure, realistic consultations on demand completely revolutionized his exam preparation. Realizing that tens of thousands of medical students, foundation doctors, and IMGs face this exact same struggle every year, he decided to refine the software and release it to the public.

Today, MedEcho AI stands as a testament to solving real-world problems through cross-disciplinary innovation. It is an educational simulator built from the ground up by a physician who understands the anxiety of the exam room, powered by code written by a developer who understands the potential of artificial intelligence.

"Our mission is simple: to ensure no healthcare professional ever fails an exam because they didn't have someone to practice with."
